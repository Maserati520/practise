Kigali Genocide Memorial

A profound place of remembrance and peace.

Remember, Learn, and Prevent

The Kigali Genocide Memorial is the final resting place for more than 250,000 victims of the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi. It serves as a place of education, documenting the history of the genocide while fostering peace and reconciliation.

Visitors can explore the indoor exhibitions that explain the history leading up to the genocide, the events of 1994, and the recovery process. The beautifully landscaped gardens provide a quiet space for reflection.
